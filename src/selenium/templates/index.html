Style Sheets essentially provide alternate views to one singular content. In our case we wish to provide alternate narratives to one singular narrative. The alternative narratives may be more meaningful to end users. This idea of a more meaningful narrative is what we are calling semantic. So, a semantic style sheet is a renarrating a existing page into something that has more meaning to an enduser.
